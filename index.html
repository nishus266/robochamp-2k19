Hi. You're looking at Robochamps.

Our aim is to encourage individuals with different skills to design and operate a robot.

ROBO RACE

It will be a competition with autonomously driving,electrically powered vehicles.The bots roll onto the racetrack and take up their positions at the start.The bot which covers the arena in minimum time is going to be the winner.

ROBO WAR

Design a manually controlled ROBOT wired within a specified dimension as mentioned in rules and regulation section.The robot that will demolish the other robot in minimum time or the robot with minimum damaged parts will be the winner.

ROBO SOCCER

Robo Soccer is a football event in which robots in wired control stand against each other how your abilities and skills in robot control,dribbling and shooting .The team should work together to control robots to bring your team to victory.

SILK ROUTE

Silk Route comprises a wall follower robot,a mobile machine,that can detect the lines drawn with black on white surface,so these robots can sense it with infrared sensors that and detect walls using ultrasonic sensors,then the processor receives the data.
